About GITAM (Deemed to be University), Hyderabad Campus

GITAM (Deemed to be University), Hyderabad Campus, established in 2009, is a premier private university located in Rudraram, Hyderabad, Telangana. GITAM Hyderabad is part of the GITAM group (founded in 1980) and is recognized for its academic excellence, modern infrastructure, and global collaborations. The university is UGC recognized and NAAC A+ accredited.

  • Established: 2009 (Hyderabad Campus)
  • Location: Rudraram, Patancheru Mandal, Sangareddy District, Hyderabad, Telangana, India
  • Campus Area: 50+ acres
  • Affiliation: UGC Recognized, NAAC A+ Accredited
  • Accreditation: AICTE, UGC, NAAC A+

The campus features state-of-the-art academic blocks, research centers, hostels, sports facilities, and a vibrant student community.
