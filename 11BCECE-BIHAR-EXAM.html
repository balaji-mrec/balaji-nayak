What is BCECE?

BCECE (Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination) is a state-level exam for admission to professional courses including Engineering, Pharmacy, and Agriculture offered by colleges in Bihar.

For Engineering admissions (B.Tech), students must appear in JEE Main and then participate in UGEAC counseling conducted by BCECEB.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects.
  • Minimum 45% marks in PCM (40% for reserved categories).
  • Must have a valid JEE Main 2025 score.
  • Must be a domicile of Bihar.

UGEAC Counseling Process

  • Register for UGEAC through BCECEB portal.
  • Upload documents and fill in choices.
  • Seat allotment based on JEE Main rank & preferences.
  • Document verification and admission in allotted college.
