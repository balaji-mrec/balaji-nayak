Admission Process

Admissions to TKRCET are based on merit through state and national-level entrance exams. The process is transparent and follows government and university norms.

üéì Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech)

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry (minimum 50% marks).
  • Entrance Exam: TS EAMCET (for Telangana state quota).
  • Counseling: Through TS EAMCET web counseling. Select "TKRCET" as your preferred option (EAMCET Code: TKRC).
  • Management Quota: 30% seats through direct admission as per guidelines.
  • Lateral Entry: Diploma holders via TS ECET (20% supernumerary seats).

üéì Postgraduate Programs (M.Tech)

  • Eligibility: B.E./B.Tech in relevant branch.
  • Entrance Exams: GATE (priority), TS PGECET (state quota).
  • Counseling: Through GATE/PGECET counseling. Choose TKRCET.

üéì MBA Program

  • Eligibility: Any bachelor's degree.
  • Entrance Exam: TS ICET.
  • Counseling: Through TS ICET counseling.
