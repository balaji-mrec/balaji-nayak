About Zoology

Zoology is a core subject for BiPC stream students, exploring animal life, structure, physiology, behavior, and evolution. The syllabus includes Animal Diversity, Human Anatomy, Genetics, and Ecology, providing a strong foundation for medical and life science careers.

Exam Preparation Tips

Understand Diagrams

Focus on drawing and labeling anatomical diagrams accurately. Practice important diagrams like heart, brain, and reproductive systems.

Memorize Terminology

Learn scientific names and biological terms. Create flashcards for quick revision of important terms and definitions.

Focus on Physiology

Understand physiological processes thoroughly. Relate concepts to real-life examples for better retention.
