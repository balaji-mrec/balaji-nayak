About Anurag University, Hyderabad

Anurag University, established in 2020 (with a legacy since 2002 as Anurag Group of Institutions), is a leading multidisciplinary private university located in Venkatapur, Ghatkesar, Hyderabad, Telangana. The university is recognized for its academic excellence, world-class infrastructure, and outstanding placement record.

  • Established: 2020 (Legacy since 2002)
  • Location: Venkatapur, Ghatkesar, Medchal–Malkajgiri district, Hyderabad, Telangana - 500088, India
  • Campus Area: 100+ acres
  • Accreditation: NAAC 'A' Grade, NBA Accredited, UGC Autonomous, AICTE Approved, NIRF Top 150
  • Affiliation: Private University (recognized by UGC)

The university offers modern laboratories, a digital library, research centers, sports facilities, hostels, and a vibrant campus life. With an annual intake of over 5000+ students, Anurag University is a top choice for higher education in Telangana and South India.

Anurag University offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across Engineering, Pharmacy, Management, Agriculture, Medical Sciences, and more. The institution is known for its research, innovation, and industry collaborations.

Admission Process

Admissions to Anurag University are based on merit through state/national-level entrance exams and the university's own entrance test (AnuragCET) for select programs. The process is transparent and follows government and university norms.

🎓 Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech, B.Pharm, B.Sc. Agri, BBA, etc.)

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with relevant subjects (PCM/PCB/Commerce/Agri as applicable).
  • Entrance Exams: TS EAMCET (B.Tech/B.Pharm), AnuragCET (for select programs), NEET (MBBS), university/other state/national exams as applicable.
  • Counseling: Through TS EAMCET/NEET/AnuragCET counseling. Select "Anurag University" as your preferred option (EAMCET Code: ANUG).
  • Management Quota: Direct admission for eligible candidates as per guidelines.
  • Fee Payment: Pay the prescribed fees to confirm admission.

🎓 Postgraduate Programs (M.Tech, MBA, M.Pharm, etc.)

  • Eligibility: Relevant bachelor's degree.
  • Entrance Exams: GATE, TS PGECET, TS ICET, AnuragCET (for select programs).
  • Counseling: Through respective exam counseling or university process.

🎓 Doctoral Programs (Ph.D.)

  • Eligibility: Postgraduate degree in relevant discipline.
  • Entrance: As per Anurag University Ph.D. admission norms.
