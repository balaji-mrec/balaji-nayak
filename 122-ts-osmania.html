About Osmania University (OU), Hyderabad

Osmania University, established in 1918, is one of India's oldest and most prestigious universities. Located in Hyderabad, Telangana, OU is accredited with an 'A++' grade by NAAC and recognized by UGC. The university spans over 1,600 acres and offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines.

  • Established: 1918 (7th oldest in India)
  • Location: Amberpet, Hyderabad, Telangana
  • Campus Area: 1,600+ acres
  • Affiliation: UGC, NAAC A++
  • Accreditation: NAAC A++, UGC 12(B), 2(F)

The iconic campus features historic buildings, modern academic blocks, extensive libraries, hostels, sports facilities, and a vibrant multicultural student community.

Admission Process

Admission to OU programs is based on merit through entrance exams and qualifying examinations.

🎓 Undergraduate Programs

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with relevant subjects (min 50% marks)
  • Entrance Exams: TS EAMCET (for professional courses), OU CET (for other UG courses)
  • Counseling: Centralized counseling by university

🎓 Postgraduate Programs

  • Eligibility: Relevant bachelor's degree with minimum 50% marks
  • Entrance Exams: OU PGCET, TS ICET (for MBA/MCA), GATE (for M.Tech)
  • Counseling: University-conducted counseling

🎓 Research Programs

  • Eligibility: Master's degree with minimum 55% marks
  • Entrance: OU Ph.D. Entrance Test (OUPET) or UGC-NET/JRF qualified
  • Interview: Mandatory after qualifying written test

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Common questions about Osmania University:

1. What is Osmania University known for?

OU is renowned for its historic legacy, academic excellence, and diverse course offerings across arts, sciences, commerce, law, and engineering.

2. Is OU a government university?

Yes, OU is a state public university established by the Nizam's government in 1918.

3. What are the top courses at OU?

Popular courses include Sciences, Arts, Commerce, Law, MBA, MCA, and Engineering through affiliated colleges.

4. How can I get admission to OU?

Through OU CET for UG courses, PGCET for PG courses, and national-level exams for professional courses.

5. What is the fee structure at OU?

Fees range from ₹5,000-25,000 per year depending on the course, with concessions for reserved categories.

6. Does OU provide hostel facilities?

Yes, OU has separate hostels for men and women with reasonable fees.

7. How are placements at OU?

OU has good placement records, especially for professional courses with average packages of ₹4-5 LPA.

8. Is OU campus safe for women?

Yes, OU has strict security measures including CCTV surveillance and women's grievance cell.

9. What extracurricular activities are available?

Numerous clubs for sports, arts, literature, debates, and social service activities.

10. Who can I contact for admission queries?

Contact the admission office at 040-27098021 or email registrar@osmania.ac.in.
