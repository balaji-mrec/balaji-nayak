📊 Data Science (DS)

🧾 Overview:

Want to turn data into decisions? Then Data Science is your path! 📈

Data Science is an interdisciplinary field that uses statistics, machine learning, and programming to analyze and interpret large volumes of data. From predicting trends to personalizing content — data scientists shape how businesses and technologies work!

📘 Core Subjects:

  • 📉 Statistics & Probability
  • 📊 Data Visualization (Tableau, Power BI, Matplotlib)
  • 🧠 Machine Learning (ML)
  • 🔢 Linear Algebra & Calculus for AI/ML
  • 📦 Big Data (Hadoop, Spark)
  • 🐍 Python & R Programming
  • 💾 Data Mining & Preprocessing
  • 🤖 Deep Learning & Neural Networks
  • 🧮 SQL & NoSQL Databases

💼 Job Opportunities:

  • 🔍 Data Scientist
  • 📊 Data Analyst
  • 🤖 Machine Learning Engineer
  • 📉 Business Intelligence Analyst
  • 🧠 AI Engineer
  • 💻 Research Scientist (AI/ML)
  • 🛠️ Data Engineer
  • 📈 Statistician

📈 Future Demand:

Data is the new oil! Every industry — from tech to healthcare to finance — needs experts who can interpret data to make smart decisions. Data Science is among the top 3 most demanded careers globally.
