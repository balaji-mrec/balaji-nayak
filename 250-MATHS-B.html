MATHS-B – GUIDE Maths-B Weightage

Mathematics-B is the foundation for advanced geometry and calculus, essential for engineering, physics, and analytical careers.

Syllabus Breakdown

First Year (Maths-IB)

  • Coordinate Geometry: Locus, Transformation of Axes, The Straight Line, Pair of Straight Lines
  • 3D Geometry: Three Dimensional Coordinates, Direction Cosines and Ratios, Plane
  • Calculus: Limits, Continuity, Differentiation
  • Applications of Derivatives: Errors and Approximations, Tangent-Normal, Rate Measure, Rolle's Theorem, Maxima-Minima

Second Year (Maths-IIB)

  • Coordinate Geometry: Circle, System of Circles, Parabola, Ellipse, Hyperbola
  • Calculus: Integration, Definite Integrals, Differential Equations
