What is JCECE?

JCECE (Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination) is a state-level entrance exam conducted for admission into B.Tech Agriculture and other allied programs. For regular B.Tech (Engineering) admission, Jharkhand accepts JEE Main scores through centralized counseling.

Eligibility for B.Tech Admission

  • Candidate must have passed 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics.
  • Minimum 45% marks in PCM (40% for reserved categories).
  • Domicile of Jharkhand is required for state quota seats.
  • Admission through JEE Main 2025 rank.

JCECEB Counseling Process

  • Visit the official JCECEB website and register using JEE Main credentials.
  • Fill choices for college & branch, then lock your preferences.
  • Seat allotment based on rank and choices filled.
  • Download allotment letter and report for document verification.
