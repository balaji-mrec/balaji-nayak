About Physics

Physics is a fundamental subject for MPC stream students, exploring the laws of nature and their applications in engineering and technology. The syllabus includes Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Waves, Optics, Electricity, Magnetism, and Modern Physics, providing a strong foundation for competitive exams like EAMCET and JEE.

Exam Preparation Tips

Understand Concepts

Focus on grasping the underlying principles and laws. Physics is conceptual—memorization alone is not enough.

Practice Numericals

Solve a variety of numerical problems daily. This builds problem-solving skills and confidence for exams.

Revise Formulas

Regularly revise key formulas and derivations. Create a formula sheet for quick reference before exams.
