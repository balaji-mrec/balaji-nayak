MECH – Mechanical Engineering

📘 Overview

Mechanical Engineering is one of the core branches of engineering that deals with the design, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems. It is the backbone of all industries including automotive, aerospace, robotics, and energy.

Mechanical Engineers are involved in innovation, problem-solving, and designing machines and systems that improve our daily lives.

📚 Core Subjects

  • ⚙️ Engineering Mechanics
  • 🔥 Thermodynamics
  • 🧪 Fluid Mechanics
  • 🔩 Machine Design
  • 🏭 Manufacturing Technology
  • 🧰 Strength of Materials
  • 📊 Heat Transfer
  • 🤖 Robotics and Mechatronics
  • 🖥️ CAD/CAM
  • 🚘 Automobile and Aerospace Engineering

💼 Job Opportunities

  • ⚙️ Mechanical Design Engineer
  • 🏭 Production/Manufacturing Engineer
  • 🔥 Thermal Power Engineer
  • 🧱 Maintenance Engineer
  • 🚀 Aerospace or Automotive Engineer
  • 🔧 Quality Control Engineer
  • 🛠️ HVAC Engineer
  • 🧠 Research & Development (R&D) Engineer
  • 💻 CAD/CAM Software Specialist
  • 🤖 Robotics/Automation Engineer

📈 Future Demand

Mechanical Engineers are in demand in all sectors—automobile, defense, energy, robotics, and smart manufacturing. With Industry 4.0, there's a huge shift towards automation and intelligent mechanical systems.

💡 Facts

  • 🔧 One of the oldest and most versatile engineering branches.
  • 🚀 Heavily involved in aerospace, automotive, and robotics industries.
  • 🛠️ Opportunities in government sectors like ISRO, DRDO, Indian Railways.
