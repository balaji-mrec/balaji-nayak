About JNTU-H

Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University - Hyderabad (JNTU-H), established in the year 1972, is one of the premier technological universities in India. Located in the heart of Kukatpally, Hyderabad, JNTU-H is renowned for its academic excellence, research contributions, and influential role in shaping technical education across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.

  • Established: 1972
  • Location: Kukatpally, Hyderabad, Telangana, India
  • Campus Area: Approximately 89 acres
  • Recognition: State University as per UGC Act
  • Accreditation: NAAC 'A' Grade, NBA Accredited Programs

The sprawling 89-acre campus is strategically located in the tech corridor of Hyderabad, offering modern infrastructure and excellent connectivity. With cutting-edge laboratories, advanced research centers, digital libraries, computing facilities, and innovation hubs, JNTU-H provides a dynamic learning environment for more than 12,000+ students across its constituent and affiliated colleges.

JNTU-H offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines of engineering, technology, pharmacy, and management. The university not only serves as an educational institution but also as an affiliation and examination authority for numerous engineering colleges across Telangana.

Over the decades, JNTU-H has produced thousands of successful alumni who have excelled in technology leadership roles, research, entrepreneurship, and public service both in India and abroad. With a strong focus on research, industry collaboration, and innovation, JNTU-H continues to be a beacon of technological education in Southern India.

Admission Process

Admission to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University - Hyderabad (JNTU-H) is based on merit through competitive entrance examinations conducted by state and national authorities. Below is a comprehensive guide to understanding the admission process:

🎓 Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech)

  • Eligibility: You must pass 10+2 (Intermediate) with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry with minimum 45% marks (40% for reserved categories).
  • Entrance Exam: TS EAMCET is mandatory for all undergraduate admissions. It is conducted by the Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE).
  • Application: Apply online for TS EAMCET between March–April. Exam usually takes place in May.
  • Counseling: After results, attend web-based counseling conducted by the Convener, TS EAMCET Admissions.
  • Seat Allotment: Based on your rank and preferences, you'll receive a seat allotment for JNTU-H or its affiliated colleges.
  • Fee Payment: Pay the prescribed tuition and other fees through online/offline mode to confirm admission.
  • Management/NRI Quota: 15% Management Quota and 15% NRI Quota seats are available in affiliated colleges. Contact the respective college directly.

🎓 Postgraduate Programs (M.Tech)

  • Eligibility: You must have a B.Tech/B.E. degree in the relevant discipline with minimum 50% marks (45% for reserved categories).
  • Entrance Exams:
    • GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering) – National level exam that gives priority in admission.
    • TS PGECET – State-level exam for candidates without valid GATE scores.
  • Counseling: Attend counseling conducted by the Convener, TS PGECET Admissions based on your GATE/PGECET rank.
  • Fee Payment: Pay the prescribed fees as per the allotment letter.

📘 MBA (Master of Business Administration)

  • Eligibility: A Bachelor's Degree in any discipline with minimum 50% marks (45% for reserved categories).
  • Entrance Exam: TS ICET – State-level exam for MBA admissions in Telangana.
  • Admission Process:
    • Apply for TS ICET online.
    • Appear for the exam and secure a rank.
    • Attend counseling and choose your preferred college/course.

📚 Ph.D. (Doctorate Programs)

  • Eligibility: Master's Degree (M.Tech/M.Pharmacy/MBA or equivalent) in the relevant subject with minimum 55% marks.
  • Admission Through:
    • JNTU-H Ph.D. Entrance Test followed by interview.
    • Candidates with valid UGC-NET/CSIR-JRF/GATE/GPAT may be exempted from the entrance test.
  • Research Areas: Multiple disciplines across Engineering, Sciences, Pharmacy, and Management.

📌 Quick Summary for Students

  • For B.Tech: Appear for TS EAMCET → Participate in counseling → Get seat allocation → Complete admission formalities.
  • For M.Tech: Appear for GATE or PGECET → Attend counseling → Get allotment → Complete admission.
  • For MBA: Appear for TS ICET → Attend counseling → Choose your preferred program.
  • For Ph.D.: Apply when notification is released → Appear for entrance test & interview → Get admission based on performance.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
