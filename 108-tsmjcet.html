About MJCET

Muffakham Jah College of Engineering and Technology (MJCET), established in 1980, is a premier engineering college in Hyderabad, Telangana. Affiliated to Osmania University and accredited by NAAC and NBA, MJCET is known for its academic excellence, research, and vibrant student life.

  • Established: 1980
  • Location: Banjara Hills, Hyderabad, Telangana, India
  • Campus Area: 30+ acres
  • Affiliation: Osmania University
  • Accreditation: NAAC, NBA Accredited Programs

The campus features modern infrastructure, advanced labs, digital libraries, innovation centers, and a dynamic learning environment for over 4,000+ students.

MJCET offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, technology, and management. The institute is recognized for its strong industry connect, research, and student-centric approach.

Admission Process

Admission to MJCET is based on merit through state and national level entrance exams. Here's a quick guide to the process:

🎓 Undergraduate Programs (B.E.)

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry (min 45% marks; 40% for reserved categories).
  • Entrance Exam: TS EAMCET (Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture & Medical Common Entrance Test).
  • Application: Apply online for TS EAMCET (March–April). Exam in May.
  • Counseling: Attend web-based counseling by TS EAMCET Convener.
  • Seat Allotment: Based on rank and preferences.
  • Management/NRI Quota: 30% seats via Management/NRI Quota. Contact college directly.

🎓 Postgraduate Programs (M.E.)

  • Eligibility: B.E./B.Tech in relevant discipline (min 50% marks; 45% for reserved categories).
  • Entrance Exams: GATE or TS PGECET.
  • Counseling: By TS PGECET Convener.

📘 MBA

  • Eligibility: Bachelor's degree (min 50% marks; 45% for reserved categories).
  • Entrance Exam: TS ICET.

📚 Ph.D.

  • Eligibility: Master's degree in relevant field (min 55% marks).
  • Admission: As per university/AICTE/UGC norms.
