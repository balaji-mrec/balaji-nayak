About IIIT Hyderabad

International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad (IIIT-H), established in 1998, is a premier autonomous university focused on information technology and computer science. Renowned for its research-driven approach, IIIT-H is consistently ranked among the top engineering institutes in India.

  • Established: 1998
  • Location: Gachibowli, Hyderabad, Telangana, India
  • Campus Area: 66 acres
  • Type: Deemed University (Autonomous)
  • Accreditation: UGC, AICTE, NAAC

The campus features modern academic blocks, world-class research centers, hostels, sports facilities, and a vibrant student community.

Courses Offered at IIIT Hyderabad

IIIT Hyderabad offers a range of undergraduate, dual degree, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in computer science, electronics, and interdisciplinary areas.

Undergraduate & Dual Degree Programs

  • B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
  • B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • Dual Degree (B.Tech + MS by Research) in CSE
  • Dual Degree (B.Tech + MS by Research) in ECE
  • Dual Degree in Computational Linguistics (CSE + MS by Research in CL)
  • Dual Degree in Computer Science and Social Sciences (CSD)
  • Dual Degree in Computer Science and Engineering & Master of Science in Exact Humanities (CSD + EHD)

Postgraduate & Doctoral Programs

  • M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering
  • M.Tech in Computer Science and Information Security
  • M.Tech in VLSI & Computer Engineering
  • M.Tech in Computer Aided Structural Engineering
  • MS by Research (various disciplines)
  • Ph.D. (various disciplines)

Admission Process

Admission to IIIT Hyderabad is highly competitive and based on national-level entrance exams and institute-specific tests.

üéì Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech & Dual Degree)

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry (min 60% marks).
  • Entrance Exams: JEE Main (Mainstream), UGEE (IIIT-H's Undergraduate Entrance Exam), Olympiad/KVPY, DASA (for foreign nationals).
  • Selection: Based on entrance exam score, interview (for some programs), and academic record.
  • Application: Apply online at the official website.

üéì Postgraduate & Doctoral Programs

  • M.Tech: GATE or IIIT-H PGEE (Postgraduate Entrance Exam) + Interview.
  • MS/Ph.D.: IIIT-H PGEE + Interview.
