Exam Preparation Tips

Understand Concepts

Focus on understanding the logic behind chemical reactions and mechanisms. Chemistry is conceptual and application-based.

Practice Equations

Write and balance chemical equations regularly. Practice conversions and calculations for numericals.

Revise Periodically

Revise key concepts, formulas, and named reactions. Make summary notes for last-minute revision.
