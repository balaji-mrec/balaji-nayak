About CMR College of Engineering & Technology, Hyderabad

CMR College of Engineering & Technology (CMRCET), established in 2002, is a reputed engineering institution in Hyderabad, Telangana. Affiliated to JNTU Hyderabad and approved by AICTE, CMRCET is known for its academic excellence, modern infrastructure, and strong placement record. The college is NAAC accredited and offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

  • Established: 2002
  • Location: Kandlakoya (V), Medchal Road, Hyderabad, Telangana, India
  • Campus Area: 10 acres
  • Affiliation: JNTU Hyderabad
  • Accreditation: AICTE, NAAC, NBA (for select programs)

The campus features modern academic blocks, well-equipped labs, digital library, hostels, sports facilities, and a vibrant student community.

Admission Process

Admission to CMRCET is based on merit through state-level entrance exams and counseling.

üéì Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech)

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry (min 45% marks; 40% for reserved categories).
  • Entrance Exam: TS EAMCET.
  • Counseling: Attend web-based counseling by TS EAMCET Convener.
  • Management Quota: 30% seats via Management/NRI Quota. Contact college directly.

üéì Postgraduate Programs (M.Tech)

  • Eligibility: B.E./B.Tech in relevant discipline.
  • Entrance Exams: GATE or TS PGECET.
  • Counseling: By TS PGECET Convener.
