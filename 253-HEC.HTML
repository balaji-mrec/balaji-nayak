HCE (History, Civics, Economics) is the ideal intermediate combination for students interested in social sciences, law, public administration, and political careers. It develops critical thinking about society, governance, and economic systems.

With HCE, you can pursue top courses like BA, LLB, Public Administration, and prepare for competitive exams like UPSC, CLAT, and more.

üìö Subjects

In the HCE stream, students study 3 major subjects:

History: Explores past events, cultures, and civilizations to understand present social structures.

Civics: Studies political systems, governance, citizenship, and constitutional framework.

Economics: Examines production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services in societies.
