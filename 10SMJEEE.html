What is SRMJEEE?

SRMJEEE stands for SRM Joint Engineering Entrance Examination. It is conducted by SRM Institute of Science and Technology for admission into its B.Tech programs at campuses in Kattankulathur, Ramapuram, Vadapalani, NCR Delhi, and other locations.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed or appearing in 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects.
  • Minimum aggregate of 60% in PCM.
  • Indian nationals, NRIs, and PIO/OCI card holders are eligible.

Exam Pattern

  • Mode: Remote Proctored Online
  • Duration: 2 hours 30 minutes
  • Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology, English, and Aptitude
  • Questions: 125 (MCQs)
  • Marking Scheme: +1 for correct answer, no negative marking

Important Dates (Tentative)

  • Application Start: November 2024
  • Phase 1 Exam: April 2025
  • Phase 2 Exam: June 2025
  • Result Announcement: 1 week after exam
  • Counseling Begins: Immediately after results

Top Courses at SRM

  • Computer Science Engineering (CSE)
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • Mechanical, Civil, AI & Robotics, Data Science, etc.

Scholarships

  • Based on SRMJEEE Rank
  • 100% to 25% fee waivers for top-performing candidates

Counseling Process

SRM conducts online counseling where candidates choose their course and campus based on merit rank. Provisional allotment is confirmed after document verification and fee payment.
