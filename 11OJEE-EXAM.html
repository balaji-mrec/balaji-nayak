What is OJEE?

OJEE (Odisha Joint Entrance Examination) is a state-level entrance exam for admission to various undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including B.Tech lateral entry. For regular B.Tech admission, Odisha conducts centralized counseling based on JEE Main scores.

Eligibility for B.Tech Admission

  • Passed 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects.
  • At least 45% marks in PCM (40% for reserved categories).
  • Must be a domicile of Odisha for state quota seats.
  • Admissions through JEE Main rank (OJEE is only for Lateral Entry B.Tech).

OJEE Counseling Process

  • Register on the OJEE counseling portal using JEE Main credentials.
  • Fill in college & branch preferences and lock choices.
  • Seat allotment based on JEE Main rank & preferences.
  • Pay fees and report to the allotted institute with documents.
