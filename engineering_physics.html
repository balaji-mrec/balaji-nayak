🧪 Engineering Physics

🔬 Overview:

Engineering Physics combines the principles of physics and engineering to design advanced materials, devices, and systems. It is a bridge between scientific research and technological innovation.

This branch empowers students with deep knowledge in quantum mechanics ⚛️, optics 🔍, materials science 🧱, nanotechnology 🧬, and electronics. Engineering physicists work on futuristic tech like semiconductors, space systems, lasers, quantum computing, and more!

CORE SUBJECTS:

Here are some of the key subjects you’ll study in Engineering Physics:

  • ⚛️ Quantum Mechanics
  • 🔋 Electromagnetism
  • 🧱 Solid State Physics / Materials Science
  • 🔍 Optics and Photonics
  • 📐 Classical Mechanics
  • 🧬 Nanoscience and Nanotechnology
  • 🖥️ Computational Physics
  • 🧲 Semiconductors and Microelectronics
  • 🌌 Astrophysics (in some programs)
  • 🚀 Applied Thermodynamics & Fluid Mechanics
