About Kakatiya University, Warangal

Kakatiya University (KU), established in 1976, is a prestigious public university in Warangal, Telangana. Recognized by UGC and accredited by NAAC, KU is known for its academic excellence, research, and diverse range of programs. The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines.

  • Established: 1976
  • Location: Vidyaranyapuri, Hanamkonda, Warangal, Telangana, India
  • Campus Area: 650+ acres
  • Recognition: UGC, AICTE, NAAC
  • Accreditation: NAAC (A+), NBA (for select programs)

The campus features modern academic blocks, research centers, digital library, hostels, sports facilities, and a vibrant student community.

Admission Process

Admission to KU is based on merit through entrance exams, state/national tests, and direct admissions for select programs.

🎓 Undergraduate Programs

  • Eligibility: 10+2 or equivalent in relevant stream.
  • Entrance Exam: TS EAMCET (Engineering/Pharmacy), TS LAWCET (Law), KU Entrance Test (for some courses).
  • Counseling: Through state-level counseling and university process.
  • Direct Admission: For select programs and management quota.

🎓 Postgraduate Programs

  • Eligibility: Graduation in relevant discipline.
  • Entrance Exams: TS ICET (MBA/MCA), GATE/PGECET (M.Tech), PGCET (Arts/Science), GPAT (Pharmacy), KU Entrance Test.
  • Counseling: By university and state authorities.
