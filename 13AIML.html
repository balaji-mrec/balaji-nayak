AIML – Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

📘 Overview

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AIML) is a specialized branch of CSE focused on creating intelligent systems that can think, learn, and adapt.

It blends computer science, data science, statistics, and domain knowledge to create smart solutions — from self-driving cars 🚗 to virtual assistants 🤖 and recommendation engines 🎯.
