What is KCET?

KCET (Karnataka Common Entrance Test) is conducted by KEA for admission into Engineering, Pharmacy, Agriculture, and other professional courses in Karnataka.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a resident of Karnataka or meet domicile requirements
  • Passed 10+2 with PCM for Engineering, PCB for Pharmacy
  • Minimum 45% marks for General, 40% for Reserved Categories

Exam Pattern

  • Mode: Offline (OMR)
  • Papers: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology
  • Each Paper: 60 Marks
  • Duration: 80 Minutes per paper
  • No Negative Marking

Important Dates (Tentative)

  • Application Start: January 2025
  • Exam Date: April 2025
  • Result: May 2025
  • Counseling: June 2025
