MPC – Maths, Physics, Chemistry

📘 Overview

MPC (Maths, Physics, Chemistry) is the most popular intermediate group for students aiming for engineering, technology, and science careers. It builds a strong foundation in analytical, logical, and problem-solving skills.

With MPC, you can pursue top courses like B.Tech, B.E., B.Sc., and prepare for competitive exams like JEE, EAMCET, and more.

📚 Subjects

In the MPC stream, students study 3 major subjects:

Mathematics: Covers Algebra, Trigonometry, Calculus, and Geometry. It sharpens logical and analytical thinking — great for engineering and computer fields.

Physics: Explains how the physical world works — including Motion, Light, Electricity, and Machines. A must for engineering, electronics, and science careers.

Chemistry: Deals with Atoms, Chemical Reactions, Acids-Bases, Organic Chemistry — useful in pharmacy, chemical engineering, and healthcare fields.
