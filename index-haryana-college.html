About Higher Education in Haryana

Haryana has a developing higher education sector with a focus on technical, management, and agricultural education. The state is increasing its number of universities and colleges, aiming to provide quality education across various disciplines. Key educational centers include Kurukshetra, Rohtak, and the NCR regions like Gurugram and Faridabad.

Admissions to many professional courses are conducted through state-level counseling based on national exams like JEE Main (for engineering), NEET (for medical), and through the HSTES (Haryana State Technical Education Society) counseling for various technical programs. The state government offers several scholarship schemes.

Major Universities & Institutions

  • Kurukshetra University (KUK) - Kurukshetra: One of the oldest state universities
  • Maharshi Dayanand University (MDU) - Rohtak: Major state university
  • Guru Jambheshwar University of Science & Technology (GJU) - Hisar: Focus on science and technology
  • Deenbandhu Chhotu Ram University of Science and Technology (DCRUST) - Murthal: State technical university
  • National Dairy Research Institute (NDRI) - Karnal: Premier agricultural research institute
  • IIT Delhi (Extension Campus) - Jhajjar: Part of IIT Delhi
  • AIIMS (All India Institute of Medical Sciences) - Rewari: Premier medical institute
  • Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology - Patiala (Near Haryana border, popular among HR students)
  • OP Jindal Global University (JGU) - Sonipat: Private university with diverse programs

Key Educational Cities/Regions

  • Kurukshetra: KUK, NIT Kurukshetra (National Institute of Technology)
  • Rohtak: MDU, Pt. B.D. Sharma University of Health Sciences
  • Hisar: GJU, Chaudhary Charan Singh Haryana Agricultural University (CCSHAU)
  • Gurugram & Faridabad (NCR): Many private engineering, management, and IT colleges; Amity University Haryana, Manav Rachna University
  • Karnal: NDRI, Dyal Singh College
  • Sonipat: OP Jindal Global University, Bhagat Phool Singh Mahila Vishwavidyalaya

Placements Overview in Haryana

  • Placements are strong in institutions located near Delhi-NCR (Gurugram, Faridabad, Sonipat) due to proximity to corporate hubs.
  • Engineering and management graduates, especially in CS/IT, see good opportunities.
  • Traditional universities also have placement cells, particularly for core engineering and science fields.
  • Leading recruiters often include: IT companies (Infosys, Wipro, TCS), manufacturing firms in the industrial belts (Gurugram, Faridabad), and service sector companies.

Placement statistics vary widely by institution and location. Refer to individual college placement reports.

Scholarships & Financial Aid in Haryana

  • Haryana Government Scholarships: Schemes for Scheduled Castes, Backward Classes, Economically Weaker Sections, and other categories.
  • Post Matric Scholarship Schemes: For SC/BC students studying in Haryana.
  • Haryana State Technical Education Society (HSTES) Schemes: May include fee waivers or scholarships for technical courses.
  • National Scholarship Portal: Access to central government scholarships applicable in Haryana.
  • University and College Specific Scholarships: Many institutions, particularly private ones, offer their own merit-based or need-based scholarships.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is HSTES counseling?

HSTES (Haryana State Technical Education Society) conducts online counseling for admissions to B.Tech, B.Arch, B.E., B.Pharmacy, and other technical diploma/degree courses in government and private institutions in Haryana based on JEE Main, NEET, and qualifying exam ranks.

Is JEE Main required for engineering admission in Haryana?

Yes, most B.Tech admissions in Haryana are based on JEE Main rank, followed by HSTES counseling.

Are there good medical colleges in Haryana?

Yes, Haryana has government and private medical colleges. Admissions are based on NEET scores, followed by counseling conducted by the Directorate of Medical Education and Research (DMER), Haryana.

Which cities in Haryana are best for higher education?

Kurukshetra, Rohtak, Hisar, and cities in the NCR region like Gurugram and Faridabad are considered major educational hubs.

How can I apply for government scholarships in Haryana?

Applications for most state government scholarships are processed online through dedicated portals like the Haryana Welfare of Scheduled Castes and Backward Classes Department portal or the National Scholarship Portal.
