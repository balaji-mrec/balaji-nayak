Rajasthan CET (REAP) 2026 Overview

The Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process (REAP) 2026, conducted by the Centre for Electronic Governance (CEG), Rajasthan, is the gateway to top engineering colleges in Rajasthan. Admissions are based on JEE Main scores and 12th marks. The process is open to students from all over India.

Detailed Subject-wise Syllabus

The REAP admission is based on JEE Main scores. The syllabus is the same as JEE Main, based on NCERT Class 11 & 12 for Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics:

Mathematics

  • Algebra: Quadratic Equations, Progressions, Binomial Theorem
  • Trigonometry: Identities, Equations, Properties
  • Coordinate Geometry: Straight Lines, Circles, Parabola
  • Calculus: Limits, Differentiation, Integration
  • Vectors & 3D Geometry

Physics

  • Mechanics: Laws of Motion, Work, Energy, Power
  • Waves & Oscillations
  • Thermodynamics
  • Electricity & Magnetism
  • Optics & Modern Physics

Chemistry

  • Physical Chemistry: Atomic Structure, Thermodynamics
  • Inorganic Chemistry: Periodic Table, Coordination Compounds
  • Organic Chemistry: Hydrocarbons, Biomolecules

Strategic Preparation Plan

Follow this 3-phase plan for REAP 2026 success:

Phase 1: Foundation Building (Jan-Feb)

  • Revise all concepts from NCERT/Board books
  • Daily practice of MCQs for each subject
  • Identify and strengthen weak areas

Phase 2: Practice & Mock Tests (Mar-Apr)

  • Solve previous years' JEE Main papers
  • Take weekly mock tests under timed conditions
  • Analyze mistakes and improve speed

Phase 3: Final Revision (May)

  • Quick revision of formulas and key concepts
  • Focus on high-weightage chapters
  • Attempt full-length mock exams
Recommended Daily Schedule (8-10 hours):
6:00-9:00 AM: Theory | 10:00-1:00 PM: Practice | 2:00-5:00 PM: Mock Tests | 6:00-8:00 PM: Revision

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common queries about REAP 2026 preparation and admission process:

What is REAP?

REAP is the Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process for admission to B.Tech/B.E. courses in Rajasthan engineering colleges. It is based on JEE Main scores and 12th marks.

Who is eligible for REAP 2026?

Candidates who have passed or appeared for 12th with Physics, Mathematics, and one of Chemistry/Biology/Computer Science from a recognized board are eligible. JEE Main score is preferred.

Is there a separate entrance exam for REAP?

No, admissions are based on JEE Main scores and 12th marks. There is no separate entrance exam for REAP.

How to prepare for REAP?

Focus on JEE Main/NCERT textbooks, solve previous year papers, take mock tests, and revise regularly. Time management and accuracy are key.

What are the top colleges through REAP?

MNIT Jaipur, RTU Kota, MBM Jodhpur, CTAE Udaipur, Govt. Women Engineering College Ajmer, and top government/private colleges in Rajasthan participate in REAP counseling.

How are seats allotted in REAP?

Seats are allotted based on JEE Main rank, 12th marks, category, preferences, and seat availability through web-based counseling.
