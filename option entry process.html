TGEAPCET-2025 Option Entry Process

Complete step-by-step guide to exercise options during TGEAPCET-2025 counselling. Learn how to select colleges and branches effectively.

You must exercise fresh options in each phase - earlier phase options will not be considered.

Step-by-Step Option Entry Process

Login to Counselling Portal

After certificate verification, login to the official TGEAPCET counselling website using your:

  • Hall Ticket Number
  • Password created during registration
  • Verification code/CAPTCHA

Login Screen Example

Navigate to Option Entry

From your dashboard, click on "Exercise Options" or "Option Entry" button to begin the process.

Ensure you're in the correct phase (First/Second/Final) as options don't carry forward.

Option Entry Button Location

Select Colleges and Branches

Use the interface to:

  1. Filter colleges by district, type (Govt/Private), or category
  2. View available branches based on your rank and category
  3. Drag-and-drop or manually arrange your preferences
Arrange options in exact order of preference - highest preference at the top.

Save and Verify Options

Before final submission:

  • Click "Save" periodically to avoid data loss
  • Review your entire preference list carefully
  • Use "Print Preview" to check your selections
  • Make any necessary changes before freezing

Verification Screen Example

Freeze Your Options

When completely satisfied with your choices:

  1. Click "Freeze Options" button
  2. Confirm your action in the pop-up window
  3. Note down the transaction ID/reference number
  4. Download and print the acknowledgement
Once frozen, options cannot be changed in that phase. Be absolutely sure before freezing.

Option Entry Strategy Tips

Prioritize Wisely

Always put your most preferred college/branch at the top, even if you think your rank may be insufficient. The system will try to allocate your highest possible preference.

Use All Available Options

Fill as many options as possible (maximum allowed). More options increase your chances of getting a seat.

Include Safe Choices

After your dream choices, include colleges/branches where your rank has high chance based on previous year cutoffs.

Consider Multiple Combinations

For colleges with multiple branches, create separate entries for different branch preferences at the same college.

Common Option Entry Questions

Can I modify options after freezing?

No, once frozen, options cannot be changed in that phase. You can only modify during the specified window before freezing.

Do options carry forward to next phase?

No, you must exercise fresh options in each phase (First, Second, Final). Earlier phase options are not considered.

How many options can I select?

There's no upper limit, but you must select at least one option. More options increase your chances of seat allotment.

What if I make a mistake?

You can modify until you freeze options. After freezing, mistakes can only be corrected in the next phase (with fresh options).
