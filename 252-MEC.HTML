MEC – Mathematics, Economics, Commerce

📘 Overview

MEC (Mathematics, Economics, Commerce) is the perfect intermediate combination for students aiming for careers in business analytics, economics, chartered accountancy, and financial management. It combines analytical skills with business knowledge.

With MEC, you can pursue top courses like B.Com, BBA, B.Sc Economics, CA, and prepare for competitive exams like CA Foundation, CLAT, and more.

📚 Subjects

In the MEC stream, students study 3 major subjects:

Mathematics: Develops analytical and problem-solving skills essential for economics and business analysis.

Economics: Studies market dynamics, economic theories, and financial systems - crucial for careers in finance and policy-making.

Commerce: Covers accounting, business studies, and financial management - foundational for business careers.
