MATHS-A – GUIDE Maths-A Weightage

Mathematics is the cornerstone of the MPC stream, developing critical thinking, problem-solving, and analytical skills essential for engineering, computer science, and data-driven careers.

Syllabus Breakdown

First Year

  • Algebra: Matrices, Determinants, Complex Numbers
  • Trigonometry: Trigonometric Equations, Inverse Functions
  • Coordinate Geometry: Straight Lines, Circles
  • Calculus: Limits, Continuity, Differentiation
  • Probability: Basic Probability, Random Variables

Second Year

  • Advanced Algebra: Vectors, 3D Geometry
  • Integral Calculus: Definite Integrals, Applications
  • Differential Equations: Formation and Solutions
  • Probability Distributions: Binomial, Poisson, Normal
  • Linear Programming: Formulation and Solutions

Expert Study Tips

Daily Practice: Solve at least 20 problems daily from different chapters
Concept Clarity: Master NCERT/Intermediate textbooks before advanced problems
Weightage Strategy: Focus more on Calculus and Algebra which carry maximum marks
Time Management: Practice timed tests to improve speed and accuracy
