CC – Cloud Computing

📘 Overview

Cloud Computing is a technology that delivers computing services like servers, storage, databases, networking, software, and more over the internet ("the cloud").

It allows businesses and individuals to access and store data remotely, scale infrastructure on demand, and reduce costs. Cloud is the backbone of digital transformation today.
