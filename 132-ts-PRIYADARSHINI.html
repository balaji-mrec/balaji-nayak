About Priyadarshini Women's College, Khammam

Priyadarshini Women's College, located in Khammam, Telangana, is a reputed institution dedicated to empowering women through quality education in engineering and technology. The college is affiliated to JNTU Hyderabad and approved by AICTE, offering a safe and supportive environment for women to pursue higher education and professional careers.

  • Established: 2009
  • Location: Near NSP Camp, Khammam, Telangana, India
  • Campus Area: 10+ acres
  • Affiliation: JNTU Hyderabad
  • Accreditation: AICTE, NAAC (applied)

The campus features modern academic blocks, well-equipped labs, library, hostels, sports facilities, and a vibrant student community focused on holistic development.

Admission Process

Admission to Priyadarshini Women's College is based on merit through state-level entrance exams and counseling.

üéì Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech)

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry (min 45% marks; 40% for reserved categories).
  • Entrance Exam: TS EAMCET.
  • Counseling: Attend web-based counseling by TS EAMCET Convener.
  • Management Quota: 30% seats via Management/NRI Quota. Contact college directly.

üéì Diploma Programs
