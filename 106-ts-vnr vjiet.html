About VNR VJIET

VNR Vignana Jyothi Institute of Engineering and Technology (VNR VJIET), established in 1995, is a premier autonomous engineering institution located in Bachupally, Hyderabad. Affiliated to JNTU-H and accredited by NAAC with 'A++' grade and NBA, VNR VJIET is known for its academic excellence, research, and vibrant campus life.

  • Established: 1995
  • Location: Bachupally, Hyderabad, Telangana, India
  • Campus Area: 25+ acres
  • Affiliation: JNTU-Hyderabad (Autonomous)
  • Accreditation: NAAC 'A++', NBA Accredited Programs

The lush green campus offers state-of-the-art infrastructure, modern labs, digital libraries, innovation centers, and a dynamic learning environment for over 6,000+ students.

VNR VJIET offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, technology, and management. The institute is recognized for its strong industry connect, research, and student-centric approach.

Admission Process

Admission to VNR VJIET is based on merit through state and national level entrance exams. Here's a quick guide to the process:

üéì Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech)

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry (min 45% marks; 40% for reserved categories).
  • Entrance Exam: TS EAMCET (Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture & Medical Common Entrance Test).
  • Application: Apply online for TS EAMCET (March‚ÄìApril). Exam in May.
  • Counseling: Attend web-based counseling by TS EAMCET Convener.
  • Seat Allotment: Based on rank and preferences.
  • Management/NRI Quota: 30% seats via Management/NRI Quota. Contact college directly.

üéì Postgraduate Programs (M.Tech)

  • Eligibility: B.Tech/B.E. in relevant discipline (min 50% marks; 45% for reserved categories).
