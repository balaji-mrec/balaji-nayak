About Stanley College of Engineering and Technology for Women

Stanley College of Engineering and Technology for Women (SCETW), established in 2008, is a leading engineering institution in Hyderabad, Telangana, dedicated to empowering women in technical education. The college is affiliated to Osmania University and approved by AICTE.

  • Established: 2008
  • Location: Chapel Road, Abids, Hyderabad, Telangana, India
  • Campus Area: 5+ acres
  • Affiliation: Osmania University
  • Accreditation: AICTE, NAAC Accredited

The campus offers modern infrastructure, well-equipped labs, digital library, and a safe, supportive environment for women students.

Admission Process

Admission to Stanley is based on merit through state-level entrance exams.

üéì Undergraduate Programs (B.E.)

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry (min 45% marks; 40% for reserved categories).
  • Entrance Exam: TS EAMCET.
  • Counseling: Attend web-based counseling by TS EAMCET Convener.
  • Management Quota: 30% seats via Management/NRI Quota. Contact college directly.

üéì Postgraduate Programs (M.E.)
