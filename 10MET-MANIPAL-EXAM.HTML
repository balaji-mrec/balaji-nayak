What is MET?

MET (Manipal Entrance Test) is conducted by Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) for admission to its engineering, pharmacy, and other undergraduate programs across Manipal, Jaipur & Sikkim campuses.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Mathematics, and English
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks in PCM
  • International students also eligible

Exam Pattern

  • Mode: Online Proctored Test (Remote or Center-Based)
  • Duration: 120 minutes
  • Questions: 60 MCQs (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, English, Aptitude)
  • No negative marking
