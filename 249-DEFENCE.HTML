Preparation Strategy

Exam Preparation: Focus on NCERT 10th/12th books for basic concepts. Practice previous 5 years' question papers. Join online test series for mock exams.
Physical Training: Start 3-6 months early. Daily routine should include running (1.6 km target), push-ups (20/day), sit-ups (30/day), and pull-ups (5/day).
Medical Readiness: Get complete health checkup before applying. Correct vision if needed. Maintain BMI between 18.5-24.9. Avoid any tattoos on prohibited body parts.
Current Affairs: Read newspapers daily. Focus on defense news, government schemes, sports events, and international relations. Make monthly notes.
Interview Prep: Practice speaking clearly and confidently. Learn about Indian armed forces history, ranks, and recent achievements. Dress formally for SSB interviews.
