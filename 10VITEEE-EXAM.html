What is VITEEE?

VITEEE stands for Vellore Institute of Technology Engineering Entrance Examination. It is a national-level entrance test conducted by VIT University for admission to its undergraduate engineering programs at Vellore, Chennai, Bhopal, and Amaravati campuses.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed 10+2 (or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology.
  • Minimum 60% aggregate in PCM/PCB (50% for reserved categories).
  • Date of birth must be on or after July 1, 2003.

Exam Pattern

  • Mode: Online (Computer Based Test)
  • Duration: 2 hours 30 minutes
  • Subjects: Mathematics/Biology, Physics, Chemistry, English, Aptitude
  • Total Questions: 125
  • Marking Scheme: +1 for each correct answer, no negative marking
