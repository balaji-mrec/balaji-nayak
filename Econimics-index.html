Welcome to the complete Economics resource specially designed for CEC/MEC students of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana Intermediate Board. This guide helps you master the 1st and 2nd year syllabus with quality study material, past question papers, and CA Foundation preparation tips.

About Economics in CEC/MEC Streams

Economics is a key subject that builds analytical thinking, policy understanding, and market insights. The syllabus includes Microeconomics, Macroeconomics, Indian Economy, and Basic Statistics—useful for careers in commerce, finance, law, and public administration.

Chartered Accountancy (CA) Pathway

Economics plays a vital role in CA Foundation. If you're aiming to become a Chartered Accountant, here's your roadmap:

  • After Intermediate: Register for CA Foundation with ICAI (no degree required immediately)
  • Subjects in CA Foundation:
    • Principles and Practice of Accounting
    • Business Laws and Business Correspondence
    • Business Mathematics, Logical Reasoning & Statistics
    • Business Economics & Commercial Knowledge
  • Economics Topics to Focus:
    • Demand & Supply Analysis
    • National Income & GDP
    • Money & Banking System
    • Public Finance & International Trade
  • Exam Pattern: 4 Papers (2 subjective + 2 objective), each of 100 marks

Exam Preparation Tips

Understand Economic Concepts

Focus on understanding economic theories and their real-world applications. Relate concepts to current economic scenarios.

Practice Diagrams

Master drawing and interpreting economic diagrams (demand-supply curves, production possibility curves, etc.) which carry significant marks.

Current Economic Affairs

Stay updated with recent economic developments, budgets, and policies as they help in answering application-based questions.
