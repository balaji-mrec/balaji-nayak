What is JEE Main?

JEE Main is a national-level engineering entrance exam conducted by the NTA for admissions into NITs, IIITs, and other top engineering institutions. It also serves as a gateway to qualify for JEE Advanced.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must have passed Class 12 (or equivalent) in 2023, 2024, or appearing in 2025.
  • No age limit (as per NTA guidelines).
  • Should have at least 75% in 12th boards for admission to NITs/IIITs (65% for SC/ST).

Exam Pattern

  • Mode: Online Computer-Based Test (CBT)
  • Papers: Paper 1 (for B.E./B.Tech), Paper 2A (for B.Arch), Paper 2B (for B.Plan)
  • Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Marking Scheme: +4 for correct, -1 for wrong (MCQs)

Important Dates (Tentative)

  • JEE Main Session 1: January 2025
  • JEE Main Session 2: April 2025
  • Results Declaration: Feb & April 2025 respectively
  • JEE Advanced Eligibility Announcement: April-End 2025

Preparation Tips

  • Focus on NCERT books first for concepts.
  • Use JEE-level practice books like HC Verma, Arihant, etc.
  • Take regular mock tests and solve previous year questions.
  • Time management and consistency are key.

Colleges Accepting JEE Main Score

  • All NITs (e.g., NIT Trichy, NIT Warangal)
  • IIITs (e.g., IIIT Hyderabad, IIIT Delhi)
  • Government-funded Technical Institutes (GFTIs)
  • Private colleges like DTU, NSUT, Amrita, etc.
