About Institute of Aeronautical Engineering (IARE), Hyderabad

Institute of Aeronautical Engineering (IARE), established in 2000, is a premier autonomous engineering institution located in Dundigal, Hyderabad, Telangana. IARE is known for its academic excellence, world-class infrastructure, and outstanding placement record. Admission Process

Admissions to IARE are based on merit through state and national-level entrance exams. The process is transparent and follows government and university norms.

üéì Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech)

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry.
  • Entrance Exam: TS EAMCET (for Telangana state quota).
  • Counseling: Through TS EAMCET web counseling. Select "IARE" as your preferred option.
  • Management Quota: 30% seats through direct admission as per guidelines.
  • Lateral Entry: Diploma holders via TS ECET (20% supernumerary seats).

üéì Postgraduate Programs (M.Tech)

  • Eligibility: B.E./B.Tech in relevant branch.
  • Entrance Exams: GATE (priority), TS PGECET (state quota).
  • Counseling: Through GATE/PGECET counseling. Choose IARE.

üéì MBA Program

  • Eligibility: Any bachelor's degree.
  • Entrance Exam: TS ICET.
  • Counseling: Through TS ICET counseling.
