TGEAPCET-2025 Counselling Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the TGEAPCET-2025 counselling process, document verification, option entry, and seat allotment.

All candidates must read these FAQs carefully before proceeding with the counselling process.

Counselling Process & Schedule

What are the important dates for TGEAPCET-2025 counselling?

The counselling process has three phases:

  • First Phase: 28-06-2025 to 22-07-2025
  • Second Phase: 25-07-2025 to 03-08-2025
  • Final Phase: 05-08-2025 to 14-08-2025
  • Internal Sliding: 18-08-2025 to 23-08-2025

Detailed schedule is available on the official website.

Is physical reporting mandatory after seat allotment?

Yes, physical reporting at the allotted college after Second Phase allotment is mandatory. You must:

  1. Submit xerox copies of all certificates
  2. Submit original Transfer Certificate (TC)
  3. Obtain acknowledgement from the college

Candidates who don't report physically will not be permitted to participate in Final Phase counselling.

Can I change my options after freezing in a phase?

No, once you freeze your options for a particular phase, you cannot modify them in that phase. However:

  • For First Phase, you can modify options during the specified window (14-07-2025 to 15-07-2025)
  • For Second and Final Phases, you must exercise fresh options
  • Earlier phase options are not carried forward

Documents & Verification

What documents are required for certificate verification?

You need to carry the following documents (original + 2 sets of photocopies):

  • TGEAPCET-2025 Rank Card
  • TGEAPCET-2025 Hall Ticket
  • SSLC or equivalent Marks Memo
  • Intermediate Marks Memos (1st & 2nd year)
  • Study/Bonafide Certificates from 6th to Intermediate
  • Transfer Certificate (Original)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income Certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhar Card
  • Passport size photographs (4 copies)
What if I miss my certificate verification slot?

If you miss your scheduled verification slot:

  • You can attend verification in subsequent phases
  • You must book a new slot when the next phase begins
  • Late verification may limit your college/branch options
  • No special arrangements will be made for missed slots

It's highly recommended to attend verification in the first available phase.

Seat Allotment & Reporting

What is internal sliding and how does it work?

Internal sliding allows you to change branches within the same college:

  • Conducted from 18-08-2025 to 23-08-2025
  • Only for candidates who have already joined a college
  • You can exercise options for other branches in your college
  • Fee reimbursement continues for eligible candidates
  • Must download new allotment order if sliding is successful
  • Must report to the new branch within specified dates
Can I cancel my seat after final phase allotment?

No, dropouts and cancellations are not permitted after Final Phase allotment. Important points:

  • After 14-08-2025, no cancellations are allowed
  • You must join the allotted college
  • Non-joining will be treated as a dropout
  • Dropouts may face penalties or restrictions in future admissions

Make careful choices during option entry in all phases.

Fees & Payments

What is the fee payment process after seat allotment?

After seat allotment in any phase:

  1. Login to your counselling account
  2. Download the allotment order
  3. Pay tuition fee online through the portal
  4. Complete self-reporting through website
  5. Report physically to college with payment receipt

Payment deadlines:

  • First Phase: 18-07-2025 to 22-07-2025
  • Second Phase: 30-07-2025 to 01-08-2025
  • Final Phase: 10-08-2025 to 12-08-2025
Is the processing fee refundable?

No, the processing fee paid during registration is non-refundable under any circumstances. This includes:

  • If you don't get a seat
  • If you don't attend certificate verification
  • If you withdraw from counselling
  • If you're not satisfied with allotted seat

The processing fee is only for participation in counselling and doesn't guarantee seat allotment.
