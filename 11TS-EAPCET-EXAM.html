What is TS EAMCET?

TS EAMCET (Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture and Medical Common Entrance Test) is conducted by JNTU Hyderabad on behalf of TSCHE for admission into engineering and other professional courses in Telangana colleges.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a citizen of India (Telangana/Andhra Pradesh domicile).
  • Should have passed or appeared for Class 12 (Intermediate) with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry.
  • Minimum marks: 45% for general, 40% for reserved categories.
