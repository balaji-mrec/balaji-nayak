IoT (Internet of Things)

🌐 Overview:

Internet of Things (IoT) is a revolutionary branch that connects the physical world with the digital world through the internet. 📶

IoT enables smart homes 🏠, smart cities 🏙️, smart vehicles 🚗, wearables ⌚, and more. Devices collect data, communicate, and make intelligent decisions with minimal human input.

CORE SUBJECTS:

Here are some important subjects you'll study in IoT:

  • 🌐 IoT Architecture & Communication Protocols
  • 📲 Embedded Systems
  • 📡 Wireless Sensor Networks
  • 🧠 Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • 🔐 Cyber Security in IoT
  • ⚙️ Microcontrollers (Arduino, Raspberry Pi)
  • ☁️ Cloud Computing for IoT
  • 🗃️ Big Data & Data Analytics
  • 🔧 Hardware Interfacing & Real-Time OS
  • 📶 Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, Zigbee, RFID technologies
