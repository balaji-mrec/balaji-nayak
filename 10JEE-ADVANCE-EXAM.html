What is JEE Advanced?

JEE Advanced is one of the most prestigious engineering entrance exams in India. It is the second phase of the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) and is conducted for admission to the Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs), IISc, and a few other top institutions.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be among the top 2.5 lakh rankers in JEE Main.
  • Should have passed Class 12 (or equivalent) in the current or previous year.
  • Maximum of two attempts in two consecutive years.
  • Age limit: General candidates should be born on or after Oct 1, 2000 (age relaxation for reserved categories).

Exam Pattern

  • Mode: Computer-Based Test (CBT)
  • Papers: Two (Paper 1 & Paper 2, both mandatory)
  • Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • Duration: 3 hours for each paper
  • Question Types: MCQs, Numerical-based, and Paragraph-type
  • Marking Scheme: Varies each year; negative marking applicable

Important Dates (Tentative)

  • JEE Main 2025 Result: April 2025
  • JEE Advanced Registration: May 2025
  • JEE Advanced Exam: June 2025
  • Result Declaration: June-End 2025
  • JoSAA Counseling: July 2025

Counseling & Admission Process

Admissions to IITs are conducted through JoSAA (Joint Seat Allocation Authority). You must register, fill your preferences, and attend counseling rounds based on your AIR (All India Rank).
