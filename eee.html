EEE (Electrical and Electronics Engineering)

🔌 Overview:

Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) focuses on the study and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. It is one of the core branches of engineering.

EEE deals with power generation ⚡, transmission, distribution, electronics, control systems, and embedded technologies. From power plants to smart grids, electric vehicles 🚗 to automation systems 🏭, EEE engineers electrify the world!

CORE SUBJECTS:

Here are some of the key subjects you will study in EEE:

  • ⚡ Electrical Circuits
  • 🔋 Electrical Machines
  • 🛠️ Power Systems
  • 📐 Control Systems
  • 🧠 Signals and Systems
  • 💡 Power Electronics
  • 🔌 Analog and Digital Electronics
  • 📲 Microprocessors and Microcontrollers
  • 🏭 Industrial Automation
  • 🌐 Electrical Measurements & Instrumentation
