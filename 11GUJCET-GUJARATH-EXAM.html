What is GUJCET?

GUJCET (Gujarat Common Entrance Test) is a state-level exam conducted by GSEB for admission to Engineering and Pharmacy courses in government and private colleges across Gujarat.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Candidate must be a resident of Gujarat or studied in Gujarat state board.
  • Passed or appearing in 10+2 with PCM (Engineering) or PCB (Pharmacy).
  • Minimum 45% marks in relevant subjects (40% for reserved categories).

Exam Pattern

  • Mode: Offline (OMR-based)
  • Papers: Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology
  • Total Marks: 120 (40 marks per subject)
  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Negative Marking: -0.25 per wrong answer
