What is AP EAMCET?

AP EAMCET (Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture and Medical Common Entrance Test) is a state-level entrance exam conducted for admission into engineering, agriculture, and medical programs in colleges across Andhra Pradesh.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a resident of Andhra Pradesh or Telangana.
  • Should have completed or appeared in 10+2 with PCM/PCB group.
  • Minimum age: 16 years (for engineering courses).
  • Must have minimum qualifying marks in 10+2 as prescribed by APSCHE.

Exam Pattern

  • Mode: Online (Computer-Based Test)
  • Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • Total Questions: 160 (MCQs)
  • Marks: 160 (1 mark each, no negative marking)
  • Duration: 3 hours

Important Dates (Tentative)

  • Application Form Release: March 2025
  • Exam Dates: May 2025
  • Result Declaration: June 2025
  • Counseling Starts: July 2025

Preparation Tips

  • Focus on NCERT syllabus for Physics, Chemistry, and Maths.
  • Practice mock tests and previous year papers regularly.
  • Time management and daily revision are key.
  • Use online resources and consult teachers for difficult topics.
