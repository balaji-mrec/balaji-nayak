About SR University, Warangal

SR University (SRU), established in 2020, is a leading private university in Warangal, Telangana. Recognized by UGC and accredited by NAAC, SRU is known for its innovative curriculum, research focus, and strong industry partnerships. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs.

  • Established: 2020
  • Location: Ananthasagar, Hasanparthy, Warangal, Telangana, India
  • Campus Area: 150+ acres
  • Recognition: UGC, AICTE, NAAC
  • Accreditation: NAAC, NBA (for select programs)

The campus features state-of-the-art academic blocks, research centers, digital library, hostels, sports facilities, and a vibrant student community.

Admission Process

Admission to SRU is based on merit through entrance exams, university tests, and direct admissions for select programs.

🎓 Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech)

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry (min 60% marks).
  • Entrance Exam: SR Engineering Entrance Exam (SRUEE), JEE Main, TS EAMCET.
  • Counseling: Through SRUEE/JEE/TS EAMCET ranks and university counseling.
  • Direct Admission: For top scorers and NRI/Management quota.

🎓 Postgraduate Programs (M.Tech, MBA, etc.)

  • Eligibility: Graduation in relevant discipline.
  • Entrance Exams: GATE/PGECET/SRUEE/CAT/MAT (as applicable).
  • Counseling: By university and state authorities.
