About VCE

Vasavi College of Engineering (VCE), established in the year 1981, is one of the premier engineering colleges in Telangana, India. Located in the vibrant area of Ibrahimbagh, Hyderabad, VCE is renowned for its academic excellence, modern infrastructure, and commitment to holistic development.

  • Established: 1981
  • Location: 9-5-81, Ibrahimbagh, Hyderabad, Telangana, India
  • Campus Area: Approximately 13.6 acres
  • Affiliation: Affiliated to Osmania University, Hyderabad
  • Accreditation: NAAC 'A++' Grade, NBA Accredited Programs, Autonomous Status

The 13.6-acre campus is located in Ibrahimbagh, offering a blend of natural beauty and modern infrastructure. With well-equipped laboratories, state-of-the-art classrooms, research centers, libraries, sports facilities, and innovation hubs, VCE fosters a vibrant learning environment for more than 4000+ students.

VCE offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various disciplines of engineering and technology. The institute emphasizes a balance of academics, industry interaction, and extracurricular activities to shape competent and socially responsible professionals.

Over the decades, VCE has produced thousands of successful alumni who hold prestigious positions in top companies and organizations across the world. With a strong placement record, industry collaborations, and a culture of innovation, VCE continues to be a top choice for aspiring engineers and technologists.

Admission Process

Admission to Vasavi College of Engineering (VCE), Hyderabad, is based on merit through competitive entrance exams conducted by the Government of Telangana and national-level bodies. Here's a detailed guide to help you understand the step-by-step process:

üéì Undergraduate Programs (B.E.)

  • Eligibility: You must pass 10+2 (Intermediate) with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry.
  • Entrance Exam: TS EAMCET is mandatory for government quota seats. It is conducted by the Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE).
  • Application: Apply online for the exam between March‚ÄìMay. Exam usually takes place in May.
  • Counseling: After results, attend TS EAMCET web-based counseling. You will choose VCE as your preferred option during this process.
  • Seat Allotment: Based on your rank and preferences, you'll receive a seat allotment letter for VCE.
  • Fee Payment: Pay the prescribed college fees through online/offline mode to confirm admission.
  • Management/NRI Quota: 30% seats are reserved. You can contact the VCE Admission Office directly for eligibility, fees, and seat availability.

üéì Postgraduate Programs (M.E.)

  • Eligibility: You must have a B.E./B.Tech degree in the related branch.
  • Entrance Exams:
    • GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering) ‚Äì National level, gives priority in admission and higher stipend-based seats.
    • TS PGECET ‚Äì State-level exam for Telangana students.
  • Counseling: Based on GATE or PGECET ranks, you'll register for the PGECET counseling process and opt for VCE.
  • Management Quota: Contact the VCE Admissions Cell for direct admission options (if applicable).

üìå Quick Summary for Students

  • If you're joining after 12th: Appear for TS EAMCET ‚Üí Choose VCE in counseling ‚Üí Join based on rank.
  • If you're applying for M.E.: Appear for GATE or PGECET ‚Üí Attend counseling ‚Üí Select VCE.
  • For Management Quota: No need to write entrance tests. Contact VCE Admissions Cell directly.
