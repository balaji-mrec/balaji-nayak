FT – Food Technology

📘 Overview

Food Technology is a field of applied science that deals with the processing, preservation, packaging, labeling, quality control, and distribution of food. It ensures food is safe, nutritious, and sustainable.

📚 Core Subjects

  • 🥗 Food Chemistry & Nutrition
  • 🔬 Food Microbiology & Safety
  • ⚙️ Food Processing & Preservation
  • 🏭 Food Plant Design
  • 🧪 Quality Assurance & Control
  • 📦 Packaging Technology
  • 🧫 Enzyme Technology
  • 🌾 Cereal, Dairy & Meat Technology

💼 Job Opportunities

  • 🥼 Food Technologist
  • 🔍 Quality Control/Assurance Officer
  • 🏭 Production Supervisor (Food Industry)
  • 🧪 R&D Scientist (Food Innovations)
  • 🧾 Food Safety Auditor / Inspector
  • 📦 Packaging Specialist
  • 🌱 Nutrition Officer

📈 Future Demand

The global food tech market is expected to grow to $350+ billion by 2027. With increasing awareness on health and food safety, this field has massive potential.

💡 Facts

  • 🥗 India is one of the largest food producers globally.
  • 🧬 Food tech blends biotechnology, chemistry, and engineering.
  • 🌱 Startups in plant-based & sustainable foods are booming.

🌍 Top Recruiters

  • 🥛 Amul, Nestle, Britannia
  • 🥫 ITC, PepsiCo, Dabur, Haldiram’s
  • 🌾 FSSAI, Patanjali, Mother Dairy
  • 🧃 Mondelez, GSK, Hindustan Unilever
  • 🚀 Food startups & research labs

🎓 Higher Studies & Certifications

  • M.Tech / MS in Food Processing, Safety, or Biotechnology
  • MBA in Agri-Business or Food Management
  • Certifications: HACCP, FSSAI, ISO22000, Nutrition Science

✨ Why Choose Food Technology?

  • ✅ Ever-growing demand for safe and nutritious food
  • ✅ Great blend of biology, chemistry, and tech
  • ✅ Broad scope in public health, R&D, startups
  • ✅ Job opportunities in both India & abroad
