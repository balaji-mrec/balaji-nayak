What is BISAT?

BISAT is an entrance examination conducted by BITS Pilani for admission to its integrated first-degree programs across BITS campuses including Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad. Itâ€™s one of the most competitive private engineering entrance exams in India.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed 10+2 from a recognized board with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
  • Minimum 75% aggregate in PCM and at least 60% in each subject.
  • Only students who have appeared or are appearing in 2025 are eligible.

Exam Pattern

  • Mode: Online (Computer Based Test)
  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, English Proficiency, Logical Reasoning
  • Total Questions: 130
  • Marking Scheme: +3 for correct, -1 for wrong answers

Important Dates (Tentative)

  • Application Opens: January 2025
  • Slot Booking: April 2025
  • Exam Date: May 2025
  • Result Declaration: June 2025
  • Counseling: June-July 2025

Preparation Tips

  • Cover NCERT thoroughly, especially for Physics & Chemistry.
  • Practice BISAT sample papers and mock tests regularly.
  • Focus on time management and logical reasoning sections.
  • Work on your English comprehension and vocabulary too.

Campuses Offering Admissions Through BISAT

  • BITS Pilani (Main Campus)
  • BITS Goa
  • BITS Hyderabad
  • BITS Dubai (through direct admission)
