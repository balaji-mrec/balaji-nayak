What is MHT CET?

MHT CET (Maharashtra Common Entrance Test) is conducted by the State CET Cell for admission into Engineering, Pharmacy, and Agriculture programs in Maharashtra colleges.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be an Indian National
  • Passed 10+2 with PCM/PCB from a recognized board
  • Minimum 45% marks in aggregate (40% for reserved category)
  • Maharashtra domicile required for state quota

Exam Pattern

  • Mode: Computer-Based Test (CBT)
  • Sections: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology
  • Total Marks: 200
  • No negative marking
  • Duration: 3 hours
