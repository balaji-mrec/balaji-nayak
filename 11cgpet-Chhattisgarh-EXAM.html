What is CG PET?

CG PET (Chhattisgarh Pre-Engineering Test) is a state-level entrance exam conducted by CG Vyapam for admission to B.Tech programs in government and private engineering colleges of Chhattisgarh.

JEE Main qualified candidates are also eligible to apply for CG state counseling along with CG PET candidates.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects.
  • Minimum 45% marks in PCM (40% for SC/ST/OBC candidates of Chhattisgarh).
  • Must be a domicile of Chhattisgarh for state quota seats.

CG PET Counseling Process

  • Register for CG PET counseling on the DTE Chhattisgarh portal.
  • Upload documents, fill choices, and lock preferences.
  • Seats allotted based on CG PET or JEE Main rank.
  • Report to allotted college with documents for admission.
