About Malla Reddy University, Hyderabad

Malla Reddy University (MRU), established in 2020 under the Telangana State Private Universities Act, is a leading private university located in Maisammaguda, Dulapally, Hyderabad, Telangana. MRU is known for its industry-focused programs, world-class infrastructure, and global collaborations. The university is recognized by UGC and accredited by NAAC, offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across engineering, management, sciences, agriculture, allied health, and more.

  • Established: 2020
  • Location: Maisammaguda, Dulapally, Hyderabad – 500100, Telangana, India
  • Campus Area: 100 acres (state-of-the-art campus)
  • Type: Private University (Deemed to be University)
  • Affiliation: UGC Recognized
  • Accreditation: NAAC
  • NIRF 2024: Ranked 101–150 (Engineering)
  • Founder Chairman: Sri. Ch. Malla Reddy

MRU offers a vibrant academic environment, modern infrastructure, and a focus on employability, research, and innovation. It is a preferred destination for students seeking quality higher education in Telangana and beyond.

Admission Process

Admissions to MRU are based on merit and entrance exams, depending on the program. The process is transparent and follows university and government norms.

🎓 Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech, B.Sc, BBA, BA, B.Com)

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with minimum 60% marks (PCM for B.Tech, BiPC for Allied Health, any stream for BBA/BA/B.Com).
  • Entrance Exam (B.Tech): JEE Main / State CET (TS EAMCET) / MRU Entrance Test.
  • Selection: Based on entrance exam score and/or merit in qualifying exam.
  • Application: Online application via official website.

🎓 Postgraduate Programs (M.Tech, MBA, M.Sc, MA, M.Com)

  • Eligibility: Graduation in relevant discipline with minimum 50% marks.
  • Entrance Exams: GATE/PGECET (M.Tech), CAT/MAT/CMAT/State CET (MBA), MRU Entrance Test (other PG).
  • Selection: Based on entrance exam score and/or merit in qualifying exam.

🎓 Doctoral Programs (Ph.D.)

  • Eligibility: Master's degree in relevant field.
  • Entrance: NET/GATE/State CET/Equivalent or MRU Entrance Test.
  • Selection: Based on entrance and interview.
