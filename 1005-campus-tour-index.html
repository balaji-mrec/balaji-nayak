General Campus Tour

Our general campus tour lasts approximately 90 minutes and covers all the highlights of our beautiful campus. You'll see:

  • Academic buildings and classrooms
  • University library and study spaces
  • Student union and dining facilities
  • Recreational and athletic facilities
  • Outdoor spaces and landmarks

Tours are led by current student ambassadors who can share their personal experiences and answer your questions.

Availability: Monday-Friday at 10am and 2pm, Saturday at 11am

×

Academic Department Tour

Our academic department tours allow you to explore facilities specific to your intended major. These tours include:

  • Department-specific classrooms and labs
  • Meetings with faculty in your area of interest
  • Discussions with current students in the program
  • Information about research opportunities
  • Career preparation resources

Please schedule at least 2 weeks in advance to allow time for department coordination.

Availability: Varies by department

×

Residence Hall Tour

Our residence hall tours give you a firsthand look at campus living options. You'll see:

  • Sample dorm rooms (single, double, and suite-style)
  • Community bathrooms and laundry facilities
  • Common areas and study lounges
  • Dining halls and meal plan options
  • Residential life staff and resources

Tours are led by resident advisors who can answer all your questions about campus life.

Availability: Monday-Friday at 1pm and 3pm
