About Commerce (CEC / MEC)

Commerce streams like CEC (Civics, Economics, Commerce) and MEC (Mathematics, Economics, Commerce) are ideal for students aiming for careers in finance, business, management, law, and government sectors. The curriculum includes subjects like Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, Civics (CEC), and Mathematics (MEC). These subjects lay a strong foundation for professional paths such as Chartered Accountancy (CA), Company Secretary (CS), Cost & Management Accountancy (CMA), B.Com, BBA, Law, and more.

Exam Preparation Tips

Understand Concepts

Focus on understanding the logic behind business transactions, economic principles, and legal concepts. Commerce is conceptual and application-based.

Practice Problems

Solve accountancy problems, case studies, and sample papers regularly. Practice calculations and formats for numericals.

Revise Periodically

Revise key concepts, definitions, and examples. Make summary notes for last-minute revision.
