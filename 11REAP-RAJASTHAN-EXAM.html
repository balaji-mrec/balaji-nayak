What is REAP?

REAP (Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process) is a centralized admission procedure conducted by the Rajasthan Technical University (RTU) for B.Tech/B.E. admissions in the state. Admissions are based on JEE Main scores or Class 12th marks.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Candidate must be a resident of Rajasthan or meet domicile requirements.
  • Passed 10+2 with Physics, Mathematics, and one optional subject (Chemistry/Biology/Computer).
  • Minimum 45% in PCM (40% for reserved categories).

Admission Process

  • Admissions via JEE Main rank or Class 12th marks (merit-based).
  • No entrance exam is conducted under REAP.
  • Online choice filling and seat allotment.
