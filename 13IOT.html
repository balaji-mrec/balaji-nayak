IoT – Internet of Things

📘 Overview

Internet of Things (IoT) is a revolutionary field where devices connect and communicate through the internet to collect and exchange data.

IoT is used in smart homes 🏡, healthcare 🏥, agriculture 🌾, industry 🏭, and even in cities 🌆 to build smart environments and automation.

📚 Core Subjects

  • 📶 IoT Architecture & Protocols
  • 📡 Wireless Sensor Networks
  • 💻 Embedded Systems & Arduino/Raspberry Pi
  • ☁️ Cloud Computing & IoT Platforms
  • 🧠 AI & Data Analytics in IoT
  • 🔐 IoT Security
  • 🧪 Sensors and Actuators
  • 🧰 Edge & Fog Computing
  • 📲 Mobile App Development for IoT
  • 🔗 Integration with Blockchain & 5G

📈 Future Demand

By 2030, over 75 billion devices will be connected via IoT. It is one of the fastest-growing sectors, powering Smart India, Industry 4.0, and global automation.

💡 Facts

  • 📱 Your phone, smartwatches, and home assistants are part of the IoT ecosystem.
  • 🌆 Many Indian cities are implementing IoT for smart traffic, waste, and lighting.
  • 💼 IoT jobs are in demand in sectors like agriculture, automotive, energy, and logistics.
