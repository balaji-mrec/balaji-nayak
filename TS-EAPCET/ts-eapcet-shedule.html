TGEAPCET-2025 COUNSELLING OFFICIAL NOTIFICATION

TGEAPCET-2025 Admissions Counselling Schedule

Complete schedule and important instructions for TGEAPCET-2025 counselling process. Stay updated with all important dates and procedures for seat allotment.

All candidates must carefully note the important dates and follow the counselling process step-by-step.

FIRST PHASE

Activity Date
Online filing of Basic Information, Payment of Processing Fee & Slot Booking 28-06-2025 to 07-07-2025
Certificate Verification for already Slot Booked candidates 01-07-2025 to 08-07-2025
Exercising Options after Certificate Verification 06-07-2025 to 10-07-2025
Freezing of options 10-07-2025
Display of Mock Seat Allocation on or before 13-07-2025
Modification of Options, if required 14-07-2025 to 15-07-2025
Freezing of Options for First Phase Seat Allotment 15-07-2025
Provisional Allotment of Seats for First Phase on or before 18-07-2025
Payment of Tuition Fee & Self Reporting through website 18-07-2025 to 22-07-2025

SECOND PHASE

Activity Date
Online filing of Basic Information for not attended candidates in First Phase 25-07-2025
Certificate Verification for already Slot Booked candidates 26-07-2025
Exercising Options after Certificate Verification (Fresh options mandatory) 26-07-2025 to 27-07-2025
Freezing of options 27-07-2025
Provisional Allotment of Seats on or before 30-07-2025
Payment of Tuition Fee & Self Reporting through website 30-07-2025 to 01-08-2025
Physically reporting at the allotted college 31-07-2025 to 02-08-2025
Updating Candidates Joining Details by college 03-08-2025
Physical reporting at allotted college after Second Phase is MANDATORY with Xerox copies of Certificates and Original TC.
Candidates who got allotment in Second Phase but didn't report will NOT be permitted for Final Phase.

FINAL PHASE

Activity Date
Online filing for not attended candidates in First & Second Phase 05-08-2025
Certificate Verification for Slot Booked candidates 06-08-2025
Exercising Options after Certificate Verification (Fresh options mandatory) 06-08-2025 to 07-08-2025
Freezing of options 07-08-2025
Provisional Allotment of Seats on or before 10-08-2025
Payment of Tuition Fee & Self Reporting through website 10-08-2025 to 12-08-2025
Reporting at College (if change of Branch/College) 11-08-2025 to 13-08-2025
Updating Joining Details by College 14-08-2025
Dropouts/Cancellations are NOT PERMITTED after Final Phase.

CENTRALIZED INTERNAL SLIDING

Activity Date
Exercising options for internal sliding (within same college) 18-08-2025 to 19-08-2025
Freezing of options 19-08-2025
Provisional Allotment of Seats on or before 22-08-2025
Downloading new allotment order, self-reporting in new branch 22-08-2025 to 23-08-2025
