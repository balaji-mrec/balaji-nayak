About Balanju Consultancy

Balanju Consultancy is an educational platform built to help Intermediate students in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana find the right academic guidance.

Whether you're studying MPC, BiPC, or preparing for competitive exams like NEET, JEE, or EAMCET, we provide curated syllabus guides, video resources, college reviews, and exam updates — all in one place.

Our mission is to make education more accessible, transparent, and tailored for students from rural and urban areas alike. We believe in empowering students to make informed decisions about their careers.

We’re constantly working on improving and expanding our content to include more branches, guides, and career advice. Thank you for supporting us!
