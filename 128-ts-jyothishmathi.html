About Jyothishmathi Institute of Technology and Science

Jyothishmathi Institute of Technology and Science (JITS), established in 1997, is a reputed engineering college in Karimnagar, Telangana. Affiliated to JNTU Hyderabad and approved by AICTE, JITS is known for its academic excellence, modern infrastructure, and strong placement record. The college is NAAC accredited and offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

  • Established: 1997
  • Location: Ramakrishna Colony, Shamirpet, Karimnagar, Telangana, India
  • Campus Area: 40+ acres
  • Affiliation: JNTU Hyderabad
  • Accreditation: AICTE, NAAC, NBA (for select programs)

The campus features modern academic blocks, well-equipped labs, digital library, hostels, sports facilities, and a vibrant student community.

Admission Process

Admission to JITS is based on merit through state-level entrance exams and counseling.

🎓 Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech)

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry (min 45% marks; 40% for reserved categories).
  • Entrance Exam: TS EAMCET.
  • Counseling: Attend web-based counseling by TS EAMCET Convener.
  • Management Quota: 30% seats via Management/NRI Quota. Contact college directly.

🎓 Postgraduate Programs (M.Tech)

  • Eligibility: B.E./B.Tech in relevant discipline.
  • Entrance Exams: GATE or TS PGECET.
  • Counseling: By TS PGECET Convener.
