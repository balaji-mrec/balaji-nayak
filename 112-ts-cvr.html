About CVR College of Engineering, Hyderabad

CVR College of Engineering, established in 2000, is a premier autonomous engineering institution located in Vastunagar, Mangalpalli, Hyderabad. Promoted by the Cherabuddi Education Society, CVR is known for its academic excellence, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and outstanding placement record.

  • Established: 2000
  • Location: Vastunagar, Mangalpalli (V), Ibrahimpatnam (M), Ranga Reddy (D), Telangana - 501510
  • Campus Area: 40 acres
  • Affiliation: Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad (JNTUH) (Autonomous)
  • Accreditation: AICTE Approved, NBA Accredited (7 UG programs, Tier 1), NAAC 'A' Grade, NIRF Ranked

The college offers modern laboratories, a digital library, research centers, sports facilities, and hostels. With an annual intake of nearly 1200+ students, CVR is a top choice for engineering aspirants in Telangana.

CVR offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various engineering disciplines. The institution is recognized for its research, innovation, and industry collaborations.

Admission Process

Admissions to CVR are based on merit through state and national-level entrance exams. The process is transparent and follows government and university norms.

🎓 Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech)

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry.
  • Entrance Exam: TS EAMCET (for Telangana state quota).
  • Counseling: Through TS EAMCET web counseling. Select "CVR" as your preferred option (EAMCET Code: CVRH).
  • Management Quota: Direct admission for eligible candidates as per guidelines.
  • Fee Payment: Pay the prescribed fees to confirm admission.

🎓 Postgraduate Programs (M.Tech)

  • Eligibility: B.E./B.Tech in relevant branch.
  • Entrance Exams: GATE (priority), TS PGECET (state quota).
  • Counseling: Through GATE/PGECET counseling. Choose CVR.

🎓 Doctoral Programs (Ph.D.)

  • Eligibility: M.E./M.Tech or equivalent in relevant discipline.
  • Entrance: As per JNTUH Ph.D. admission norms.
