What is WBJEE?

WBJEE (West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination) is conducted by the West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board for admission to engineering, pharmacy, and architecture programs in West Bengal.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Indian citizenship & domicile of West Bengal (for state quota)
  • Passed 10+2 with Physics & Math + Chemistry/Biology/CS
  • Minimum 45% marks (40% for SC/ST/OBC) in PCM
  • Age limit: Minimum 17 years
