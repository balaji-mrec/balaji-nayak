Document Submission Process

  1. Certificate Verification: Submit all original documents + photocopies at Help Line Center
  2. After Allotment: Carry same documents + allotment order to college
  3. College Reporting: Submit original TC + 1 set of photocopies at allotted college
  4. Retain: Keep one set of photocopies for your records
Original Transfer Certificate (TC) will be retained by the allotted college. Obtain acknowledgement.
