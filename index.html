About MVSR Engineering College

MVSR Engineering College (Maturi Venkata Subba Rao Engineering College), established in 1981, is one of the premier private engineering colleges in Telangana. Located in Nadergul, Hyderabad, MVSR is known for its academic excellence, industry relevance, and vibrant campus life.

  • Established: 1981
  • Location: Nadergul, Hyderabad, Telangana, India
  • Campus Area: Sprawling campus with modern infrastructure
  • Affiliation: Osmania University
  • Accreditation: AICTE Approved, NBA Accredited (CSE, ECE, EEE, IT, Mechanical, Civil), NIRF Ranked

The college offers state-of-the-art laboratories, digital library, research centers, sports facilities, and hostels. With a yearly intake of around 1000+ students, MVSR has a strong reputation for producing industry-ready graduates and successful alumni.

MVSR offers undergraduate, postgraduate, MBA, and Ph.D. programs in various engineering disciplines. The college is recognized as a research center by Osmania University for Ph.D. in CSE, ECE, and Mechanical Engineering.

Admission Process

Admissions to MVSR Engineering College are based on merit through state and national-level entrance exams. The process is transparent and follows government and university norms.

🎓 Undergraduate Programs (B.E.)

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry.
  • Entrance Exam: TS EAMCET (for Telangana state quota).
  • Counseling: Through TS EAMCET web counseling. Select "MVSR Engineering College" as your preferred option (EAMCET Code: MVSR).
  • Management Quota: Direct admission for eligible candidates as per guidelines.
  • Fee Payment: Pay the prescribed fees to confirm admission.

🎓 Postgraduate Programs (M.E./M.Tech)

  • Eligibility: B.E./B.Tech in relevant branch.
  • Entrance Exams: GATE (priority), TS PGECET (state quota).
  • Counseling: Through GATE/PGECET counseling. Choose MVSR Engineering College.

🎓 MBA Program

  • Eligibility: Any bachelor's degree.
  • Entrance Exam: TS ICET (state quota).
  • Counseling: Through TS ICET counseling. Choose MVSR Engineering College.

🎓 Doctoral Programs (Ph.D.)

  • Eligibility: M.E./M.Tech or equivalent in relevant discipline.
  • Entrance: As per Osmania University Ph.D. admission norms.
