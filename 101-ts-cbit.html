About Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Technology (MGIT), Hyderabad

Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Technology (MGIT), established in 1997, is a premier engineering institution in Hyderabad, Telangana. Affiliated to JNTU Hyderabad and approved by AICTE, MGIT is renowned for its academic excellence, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and strong placement record.

  • Established: 1997
  • Location: Kokapet, Gandipet, Hyderabad
  • Campus Area: 30+ acres
  • Affiliation: JNTU Hyderabad
  • Accreditation: AICTE, NAAC, NBA

MGIT offers comprehensive undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology.

Admission Process

Get admitted to MGIT through state-level entrance exams and counseling process.

🎓 B.Tech Admissions

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with PCM (min 45% marks)
  • Entrance Exam: TS EAMCET
  • Counseling: Web-based counseling
  • Management Quota: 30% seats available

🎓 M.Tech Admissions

  • Eligibility: B.E./B.Tech in relevant field
  • Entrance Exams: GATE or TS PGECET
  • Counseling: TS PGECET Convener

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about MGIT admissions and campus life:

What is MGIT known for?
MGIT is a reputed engineering college in Hyderabad, known for its strong academics, modern infrastructure, and excellent placement record. It's affiliated to JNTU Hyderabad and AICTE approved.
How to get admission in MGIT?
Admission is through TS EAMCET for B.Tech and GATE/PGECET for M.Tech, followed by counseling. Management quota seats are also available.
What are the fees at MGIT?
Convener quota: ₹1,35,000/year, Management quota: ₹2,00,000/year approximately. Additional charges for hostel, transport, and other facilities.
Does MGIT provide hostel facilities?
Yes, separate hostels for boys and girls with mess facilities, Wi-Fi, and 24/7 security are available.
How are placements at MGIT?
MGIT has excellent placement record with 80% placement rate, highest package of ₹25 LPA, and average package of ₹5.2 LPA. Top companies regularly recruit from campus.
What courses does MGIT offer?
B.Tech in CSE, AI&ML, ECE, EEE, IT, Mechanical, Civil, MME, Mechatronics and M.Tech programs in various specializations.
Is there transport facility?
Yes, college buses operate on various routes. The campus is also well-connected by public transport.
What extracurricular activities are available?
Technical clubs, cultural societies, sports facilities, annual fests, and various student organizations are active on campus.
