About CBIT

Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT), established in the year 1979, is one of the premier engineering colleges in Telangana, India. Located in the serene surroundings of Gandipet, Hyderabad, CBIT is renowned for its academic excellence, rich legacy, and commitment to holistic development.

  • Established: 1979
  • Location: Gandipet, Hyderabad, Telangana, India
  • Campus Area: Approximately 50 acres
  • Affiliation: Affiliated to Osmania University
  • Accreditation: NAAC 'A' Grade, NBA Accredited Programs

The sprawling 50-acre campus is strategically located near Osman Sagar Lake, offering a blend of natural beauty and modern infrastructure. With well-equipped laboratories, state-of-the-art classrooms, research centers, libraries, sports facilities, and innovation hubs, CBIT fosters a vibrant learning environment for more than 5000+ students.

CBIT offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines of engineering, technology, and management. The institute emphasizes a balance of academics, industry interaction, and extracurricular activities to shape competent and socially responsible professionals.

Over the decades, CBIT has produced thousands of successful alumni who hold prestigious positions in top companies and organizations across the world. With a strong placement record, industry collaborations, and a culture of innovation, CBIT continues to be a top choice for aspiring engineers and technologists.

Admission Process

Admission to Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT), Hyderabad, is based on merit through competitive entrance exams conducted by the Government of Telangana and national-level bodies. Here's a detailed guide to help you understand the step-by-step process:

🎓 Undergraduate Programs (B.E. / B.Tech)

  • Eligibility: You must pass 10+2 (Intermediate) with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry.
  • Entrance Exam: TS EAMCET is mandatory for government quota seats. It is conducted by the Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE).
  • Application: Apply online for the exam between March–May. Exam usually takes place in May.
  • Counseling: After results, attend TS EAMCET web-based counseling. You will choose CBIT as your preferred option during this process.
  • Seat Allotment: Based on your rank and preferences, you'll receive a seat allotment letter for CBIT.
  • Fee Payment: Pay the prescribed college fees through online/offline mode to confirm admission.
  • Management/NRI Quota: 30% seats are reserved. You can contact the CBIT Admission Office directly for eligibility, fees, and seat availability.

🎓 Postgraduate Programs (M.E. / M.Tech)

  • Eligibility: You must have a B.E./B.Tech degree in the related branch.
  • Entrance Exams:
    • GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering) – National level, gives priority in admission and higher stipend-based seats.
    • TS PGECET – State-level exam for Telangana students.
  • Counseling: Based on GATE or PGECET ranks, you’ll register for the PGECET counseling process and opt for CBIT.
  • Management Quota: Contact the CBIT Admissions Cell for direct admission options (if applicable).

📘 MBA (Master of Business Administration)

  • Eligibility: A Bachelor's Degree in any stream (Engineering, Commerce, Arts, etc.).
  • Entrance Exam: TS ICET – State-level exam for MBA admissions in Telangana.
  • Admission Process:
    • Apply for ICET online.
    • Write the exam and get a rank.
    • Attend TS ICET counseling and choose CBIT as your preferred college.
  • Management Seats: Limited seats available under the Management quota for eligible students.

📚 Ph.D. (Doctorate Programs)

  • Eligibility: Master's Degree (M.E./M.Tech/MBA or equivalent) in the relevant subject.
  • Admission Through:
    • Osmania University Ph.D. Entrance Test
    • OR qualifying exams like UGC-NET, CSIR-JRF, GATE (for Engineering streams).
  • Research Areas: Engineering disciplines, Applied Sciences, and Management.

📌 Quick Summary for Students

  • If you're joining after 12th: Appear for TS EAMCET → Choose CBIT in counseling → Join based on rank.
  • If you're applying for M.Tech: Appear for GATE or PGECET → Attend counseling → Select CBIT.
  • For MBA: Appear for TS ICET → Attend counseling → Select CBIT MBA program.
  • For Management Quota: No need to write entrance tests. Contact CBIT Admissions Cell directly.

Campus Facilities at CBIT

CBIT offers a vibrant, well-equipped campus designed to support academics, innovation, and student well-being. Below is a comprehensive list of the core facilities available:

  • Smart Classrooms: Digitally enabled with projectors, internet, and ergonomic seating.

  • Innovation Centers: In-house incubation & startup cell, Microsoft Innovation Center, AICTE Idea Lab.

💻 Digital & Technical Resources

  • High-Speed Wi-Fi: Campus-wide internet access (1 Gbps bandwidth).
  • Computer Centers: Over 1,000 systems with updated software tools.

  • Hostel Facilities: Separate hostels for boys and girls with 24/7 security, Wi-Fi, mess & laundry.

⚽ Sports & Recreation

  • Sports Complex: Cricket ground, football field, volleyball & basketball courts, indoor badminton hall.
  • Gymnasium: Equipped fitness center accessible to students & staff.
