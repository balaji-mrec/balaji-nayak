English Exam Preparation Tips

Read Widely

Regular reading improves vocabulary, grammar, and writing style. Read newspapers, magazines, and quality literature.

Practice Writing

Write essays, letters, and reports regularly. Focus on structure, coherence, and appropriate style for different formats.

Speak English Daily

Practice speaking English with friends or in front of a mirror. Record yourself to improve pronunciation and fluency.
