About Vaagdevi College of Engineering, Warangal

Vaagdevi College of Engineering (VCE), established in 1998, is a leading engineering institution in Warangal, Telangana. Affiliated to JNTU Hyderabad and approved by AICTE, VCE is known for its academic excellence, modern infrastructure, and strong placement record. The college is NAAC accredited and offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

  • Established: 1998
  • Location: Bollikunta, Khila Dist, Warangal, Telangana, India
  • Campus Area: 25+ acres
  • Affiliation: JNTU Hyderabad
  • Accreditation: AICTE, NAAC, NBA (for select programs)

The campus features modern academic blocks, well-equipped labs, digital library, hostels, sports facilities, and a vibrant student community.

Courses Offered at Vaagdevi College of Engineering

Vaagdevi College offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology.

Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech – 4 Years)

  • B.Tech in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)

Admission Process

Admission to Vaagdevi College is based on merit through state-level entrance exams and counseling.

🎓 Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech)

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry (min 45% marks; 40% for reserved categories).
  • Entrance Exam: TS EAMCET.
  • Counseling: Attend web-based counseling by TS EAMCET Convener.
  • Management Quota: 30% seats via Management/NRI Quota. Contact college directly.

🎓 Postgraduate Programs (M.Tech)

  • Eligibility: B.E./B.Tech in relevant discipline.
  • Entrance Exams: GATE or TS PGECET.
  • Counseling: By TS PGECET Convener.
