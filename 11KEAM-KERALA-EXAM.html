What is KEAM?

KEAM (Kerala Engineering Architecture Medical) is the entrance exam conducted by CEE Kerala for admission into engineering, pharmacy, architecture, and medical-related courses in the state.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Kerala native or hold Keralite/non-Keralite Category I or II status
  • Must have passed 10+2 with PCM for Engineering, PCB for Pharmacy/Medical
  • Minimum 45% in PCM/PCB for General (40% for Reserved)

Exam Pattern

  • Mode: Offline (OMR-based)
  • Paper 1: Physics & Chemistry
  • Paper 2: Mathematics
  • Total Marks: 960 (Each paper 480)
  • Negative Marking: -1 for wrong answer

Important Dates (Tentative)

  • Application Start: March 2025
  • Exam Date: May 2025
  • Result: June 2025
  • Counseling: July 2025

KEAM Counseling Process

  • Register at CEE Kerala portal
  • Upload documents & verify
  • Fill web options (college & branch)
  • Allotment Rounds (3 phases + spot round)
  • Join allotted college with fee payment

Preparation Tips

  • Focus on Kerala SCERT syllabus & NCERT Class 11-12
  • Solve past 5-year KEAM question papers
  • Time yourself during mock tests
  • Revise tricky concepts regularly
